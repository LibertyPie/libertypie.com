Faq

Still have some questions?, use the faq to find answers

What is LibertyPie Ecosystem
LibertyPie is an ecosystem of DeFi protocols consisting of a decentralized P2P exchange, a dex aggregator & a cross-chain asset bridge.
What is LibertyPie P2P Exchange
LibertyPie P2P is a non-custodial, permissionless & autonomous decentralized P2P exchange protocol based on smart contracts.
What is XPIE
XPIE is the deflationary governance token of LibertyPie project.
How will you manage to handle Bitcoin on EVM based blockchain
There are numerous projects such as RenVm which bridge Bitcoin to the Ethereum and Binance Smart chain network.
Does LibertyPie Support Staking
Yes, the network supports staking either by becoming a guardian or by using the DeFi vault.
